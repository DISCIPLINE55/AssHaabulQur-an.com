Daily Duas & Supplications🤲

Dua Before Sleeping

اللَّهُمَّ بِاسْمِكَ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

Allahumma bismika amutu wa ahya

O Allah, in Your name I die and live.

Dua for Forgiveness

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَتُبْ عَلَيَّ إِنَّكَ أَنتَ التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيمُ

Rabbighfir li wa tub 'alayya innaka anta At-Tawwab Ar-Raheem

My Lord, forgive me and accept my repentance. Indeed, You are the Accepting of Repentance, the Merciful.
